# 🔗 Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM)

This RTM demonstrates clear traceability between **Test Scenarios (TS)**,  
**Test Cases (TC)**, and **Bug Reports (BUG)** for the **Logistics & Transportation Platform**.

Testing was performed in a structured, requirement-driven manner, ensuring
full coverage of critical logistics, booking, localization, and delivery flows.

## 🎯 Purpose

The RTM ensures that:

- Each **business requirement** is covered by a Test Scenario  
- Every Scenario is validated through a Test Case  
- All detected Bugs are traceable to specific test executions  
- No critical functionality remains untested  
- QA coverage is transparent, auditable, and portfolio-ready
