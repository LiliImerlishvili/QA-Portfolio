# 🐞 BUG-007 – Lesson Video Freezes on Safari Browser

**Priority:** Medium  
**Severity:** Major  

## Description
Lesson video freezes during playback on Safari browser.

## Preconditions
- User uses Safari browser
- Lesson video available

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open lesson in Safari
2. Play video for several minutes

## Actual Result
Video freezes and stops playback.

## Expected Result
Video should play smoothly without interruptions.
