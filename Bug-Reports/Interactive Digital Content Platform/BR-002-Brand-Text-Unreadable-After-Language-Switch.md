# BR-002 — Brand Text Becomes Unreadable After Switching Language

## Summary

The platform brand text becomes difficult to read after switching to another language because of insufficient contrast.

## Environment

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Environment | Staging |
| Device | iMac |
| Operating System | macOS Tahoe 26.3.1 |
| Browser | Google Chrome 146 |
| Reproducibility | 100% |

## Severity

High

## Priority

High

## Preconditions

- The relevant feature is available.
- The user has the required access level.

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open the platform.
2. Switch to another language.
3. Navigate to a main content page.
4. Observe the brand or logo text.

## Actual Result

The text becomes visually unclear because the foreground and background colors have insufficient contrast.

## Expected Result

The brand text should remain readable in every supported language and meet accessibility contrast requirements.

## User or Business Impact

Brand identity and navigation clarity are reduced.
