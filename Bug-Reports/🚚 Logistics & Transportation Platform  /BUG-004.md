# 🐞 BUG-004 – Delivery status does not update in real time

**Priority:** Medium  
**Severity:** Major  

## Description
Delivery status updates are not reflected automatically on the tracking page.

## Preconditions
- Active delivery exists

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the delivery tracking page
2. Wait for status update

## Actual Result
The status remains outdated until the page is refreshed.

## Expected Result
The delivery status should update in real time.
