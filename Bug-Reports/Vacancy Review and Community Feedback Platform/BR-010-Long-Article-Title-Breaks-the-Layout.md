# BR-010 — Long Article Title Breaks the Layout

## Summary

A long article title overflows its field and disrupts the page layout.

## Environment

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Environment | Staging |
| Device | iMac |
| Operating System | macOS Tahoe 26.5.1 |
| Browser | Google Chrome 149 |
| Reproducibility | 100% |

## Severity

Medium

## Priority

High

## Preconditions

- The user has access to the relevant flow.
- The required platform data is available.

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open article creation.
2. Enter a long title.
3. Save or preview the article.

## Actual Result

The title overflows the field and breaks the surrounding layout.

## Expected Result

The title should wrap or truncate within the available space without breaking the layout.

## User or Business Impact

Content presentation becomes visually broken.
