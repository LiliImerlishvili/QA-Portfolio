# TC-RE-008 - Contact Workflow From Listing Page

**Priority:** Medium

## Preconditions
- User is on the listings page
- A contact CTA or quick action is available (if applicable)

## Steps
1. Click the contact CTA from a listing card
2. Enter valid data in contact form (if displayed)
3. Submit the request

## Expected Result
- User is routed to correct contact flow (modal or page)
- Submission works and confirmation is displayed
- No incorrect redirects occur
