# TC-GRID-001 – Verify Footer Blog Grid Layout and Pagination

**Purpose:**  
To verify that the footer Blog Grid layout and pagination display correctly without overlaps.

**Related Bug:** UI-GRID-022  
**Module / Feature:** Blog / Footer Grid  
**Priority:** Medium  

**Preconditions:**  
- Blog posts exist.  
- Grid layout and pagination are enabled.

**Environment:**  
- Device: iMac  
- Browser: Chrome 142.x  

---

### Steps:
1. Open homepage.  
2. Scroll down to the footer area.  
3. Locate the "Blog" grid section.  
4. Observe alignment of cards on multiple rows.  
5. Check if pagination (page numbers / next/prev) is visible and correctly placed.  

---

### Expected Result:
- All blog cards are aligned in a stable grid.  
- No overlapping text or images.  
- Pagination is visible and aligned under the grid.  

---

### Status:
❌ Failed
