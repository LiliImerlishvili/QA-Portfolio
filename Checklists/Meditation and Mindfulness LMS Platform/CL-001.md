# ☑️ Course Access & Gated Content Checklist

- [ ] Free courses are accessible without subscription
- [ ] Paid courses are locked for non-subscribed users
- [ ] Subscription prompt is displayed for locked lessons
- [ ] Access is granted immediately after successful payment
- [ ] Direct URL access to locked lessons is blocked
- [ ] Course access persists after page refresh
- [ ] Course access persists after logout/login
