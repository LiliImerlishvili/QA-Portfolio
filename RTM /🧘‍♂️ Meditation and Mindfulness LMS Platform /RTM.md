**Test Cases (TC)**, and **Bug Reports (BUG)** for the  
**Meditation and Mindfulness LMS Platform (NDA Project)**.

It confirms that testing was performed in a structured, requirement-driven way  
with full coverage of critical learning, payment, localization, compliance, and tracking flows.

## 🎯 Purpose

The RTM ensures that:

- Each **business requirement** is covered by at least one Test Scenario  
- Every Scenario is validated through a structured Test Case  
- All reported Bugs are traceable to specific test execution evidence  
- QA coverage is transparent, auditable, and portfolio-ready
