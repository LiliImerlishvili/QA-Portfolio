# TS-001 — Registration and Onboarding Flow

## Objective

Verify that a new user can complete registration through a clear and validated onboarding process.

## Scenario

1. Open registration.
2. Enter a valid email.
3. Complete email validation.
4. Create a password.
5. Complete the remaining profile fields.
6. Submit registration.
7. Verify successful account creation.

## Expected Result

- The flow is clearly divided into logical steps.
- Validation feedback is displayed.
- The account is created successfully.
- The user reaches the expected post-registration screen.
