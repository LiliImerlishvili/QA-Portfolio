**ID:** BUG-001  
**Priority:** High  

## Description
On mobile devices, the homepage slider overlaps the primary Call-To-Action button, making it partially unclickable.

## Preconditions
- User is on mobile device
- Homepage is opened

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the homepage on mobile
2. Scroll to the hero section
3. Observe slider and CTA positioning

## Actual Result
CTA button is partially covered by the slider.

## Expected Result
CTA button should be fully visible and clickable.
