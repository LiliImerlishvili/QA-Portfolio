**Severity:** Medium  
**Environment:** Chrome · Safari · Firefox · Edge  

---

## Summary  
Favorites button does not consistently add products to favorites.

---

## Steps  
1. Click Favorites  
2. Check Favorites list  

---

## Actual  
Product is not added.

---

## Expected
