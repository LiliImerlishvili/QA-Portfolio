# TC-SIMBR-001 – Similar Products From Same Brand Only

**Purpose:**  
To verify “Similar products from this brand” shows items from the same brand.

**Related Bug:** UI-SIMBRAND-118  
**Module / Feature:** Recommendations – Brand-Based  
**Priority:** Medium  

---

### Steps:
1. Open a branded product (e.g., Calvin Klein product).  
2. Scroll to “Similar products from this brand” block.  
3. Check brand names of all items.

---

### Expected Result:
- All displayed items belong to the same brand as main product.
