**Bug ID:** BUG-003  
**Priority:** High  
**Severity:** Critical  
**Status:** Open  

## Description
Employers can publish job postings without providing a job description, resulting in incomplete job listings.

## Preconditions
- Employer is logged in
- Job creation page is accessible

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open Job Creation page
2. Fill all fields except Job Description
3. Click "Publish Job"

## Actual Result
Job posting is published successfully.

## Expected Result
System should block submission and require job description.

## Environment
- Browser: Chrome 121
- OS: macOS
- Environment: Staging
