# UI-PREV-041 – Closing Fullscreen Preview Opens Wrong Product

**Severity:** Medium  
**Environment:** macOS · Chrome  

---

## Summary  
Closing image preview triggers redirect to wrong product.

---

## Steps  
1. Open product  
2. Open fullscreen image  
3. Close preview  

---

## Actual  
Redirects to another product.

---

## Expected  
Stay on the same product.

---

## Notes  
Incorrect ID reference in preview close function.
