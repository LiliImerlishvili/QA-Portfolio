# CAT-BRND-007 – Brand “Levi’s” Redirects to Wrong Category

**Severity:** High  
**Environment:** Windows 10 · Chrome 141  
**Reproducibility:** 100 percent

---

## Summary  
Clicking “Levi’s” opens unrelated brand category items (Jeep, ALOHAS, etc.).

---

## Steps  
1. Open Brands section  
2. Click “Levi’s”  
3. Observe product list  

---

## Actual  
Products belong to unrelated brands.

---

## Expected  
Only Levi’s products should appear.

---

## Notes  
Incorrect brand–category mapping.
