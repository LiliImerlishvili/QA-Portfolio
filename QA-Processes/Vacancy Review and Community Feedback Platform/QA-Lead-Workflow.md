# QA Lead Workflow

## Responsibilities
- Define test scope and risk priorities
- Coordinate testing activities
- Assign and review QA tasks
- Review bug quality and reproducibility
- Maintain test cases, checklists, scenarios, and RTM
- Coordinate retesting and regression
- Communicate release risks and quality status
- Support testers with standards and feedback

## Regression Process
1. Review changed functionality.
2. Identify impacted areas.
3. Select linked test cases.
4. Execute targeted regression.
5. Record results.
6. Communicate blockers and residual risks.
