# TS-RE-001 - Browse Listings and Open Property

## Goal
Verify that users can browse property listings and open a property details page without errors or broken navigation.

## Preconditions
- Listings page is available
- At least one property card is visible

## Flow (High-level)
1. Open listings page
2. Scroll through listings
3. Open a property from the list
4. Verify details page loads successfully
