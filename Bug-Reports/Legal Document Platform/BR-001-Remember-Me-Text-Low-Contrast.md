# BR-001 — Remember Me Text Has Insufficient Contrast

## Summary

The Remember Me label on the login screen has insufficient contrast and is difficult to read.

## Environment

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Environment | Staging |
| Device | iMac |
| Operating System | macOS Tahoe 26.3.1 |
| Browser | Google Chrome 146 |
| Reproducibility | 100% |

## Severity

Medium

## Priority

Normal

## Preconditions

- The user is authenticated unless otherwise stated.
- The relevant feature is available.

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open the login page.
2. Locate the Remember Me checkbox and label.
3. Compare the label contrast with surrounding form controls.

## Actual Result

The text appears too light against the background and is difficult to distinguish.

## Expected Result

The label should meet accessibility contrast requirements and remain clearly readable.

## User or Business Impact

Users with visual impairments may struggle to identify the control.

## Attachment

Evidence available in the original defect management system.
